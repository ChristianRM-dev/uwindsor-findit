Privacy

FindIt stores the information needed to help users report items, verify ownership, and review claim activity. This page explains, at a project level, what user data is handled inside the platform.

What we store

  • Account information such as name, email, student ID, and phone number
  • Item report details, uploaded photos, and claim proof files
  • Activity records such as searches, page visits, claims, and messages

Why we store it

  • To connect owners with found items
  • To support claim review and fraud prevention
  • To provide user history and notification features
This is a course project privacy summary rather than a production legal policy.
