About FindIt

FindIt is a campus lost-and-found platform built for the University of Windsor community. It gives students and staff a simpler way to report missing belongings, search recent reports, and reconnect owners with their items faster.

What you can do

  • Report lost and found items
  • Browse and search public listings
  • Claim items with proof of ownership
  • Message item reporters directly

Why it matters

Losing something on campus is stressful. FindIt helps reduce that friction by keeping the reporting, searching, and claim process in one place instead of scattered across messages or bulletin boards.

1. Report

Create a lost or found item report with key details such as category, location, and description.

2. Search

Browse recent listings or search by keyword to quickly narrow down possible matches across campus.

3. Reconnect

Submit a claim and use the built-in messaging flow to coordinate with the reporter safely.

Trust and safety

FindIt is designed to encourage responsible recovery by supporting proof-based claims and direct, item-specific communication. This helps protect users while improving the chances of returning belongings to the right owner.

Who it is for

Students, staff, and visitors who need a clear, campus-friendly process for recovering lost items.
