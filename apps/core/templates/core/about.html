About FindIt

FindIt is a campus lost and found platform for reporting items, browsing recent reports, and reconnecting owners with their belongings.

What you can do

  • Report lost and found items
  • Browse and search public listings
  • Claim items with proof of ownership
  • Message item reporters directly

Who it is for

Students, staff, and visitors who need a simple way to report or recover belongings on campus.
