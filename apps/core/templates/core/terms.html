Terms of Use

FindIt is designed for responsible lost-and-found reporting within the University of Windsor community. By using the site, users are expected to provide truthful information and respectful communication.

User expectations

  • Provide accurate item and claim details
  • Upload proof only for legitimate ownership verification
  • Use messaging and claims respectfully

Platform rules

  • False claims may be rejected or flagged by staff/admin users
  • Public listings can be hidden or reviewed when needed
  • Course-project deployments may reset sample data during development
These terms summarize expected use for the course project and do not replace an institutional legal agreement.
